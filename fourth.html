About Elite 11

Elite 11 is a football shop built for players who care about performance, comfort, and confidence on the pitch. We deliver quality football equipment at affordable prices — because great gear should be available to everyone.

We started in 2025 with one simple goal: make it easier for players to find the right football equipment without paying crazy prices. From boots and balls to gloves and accessories, we focus on products that help you perform at your best.

Made by a Player

Elite 11 was created by a football player — someone who understands the game from inside the pitch. We know what players look for, what matters during training, and what performs under match pressure.

Whether you are training daily, playing weekend matches, or just starting your football journey, the right equipment makes a real difference.

What We Believe

Football is more than just a game. It is discipline, teamwork, ambition, and constant improvement. We believe every player deserves access to equipment that supports their progress and confidence.

Our Promise

Our promise is simple: quality products, fair pricing, and a smooth shopping experience. As Elite 11 continues to grow, we aim to expand our collections, improve our service, and stay focused on what matters most — the players.

Thank you for being part of Elite 11. We are here to help you step onto the pitch prepared, confident, and ready to perform.
